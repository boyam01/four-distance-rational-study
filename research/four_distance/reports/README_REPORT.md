# Four Distance Reports

This directory contains reproducible reports generated by the exact
four-distance research harness.

Primary reports:

- `delta_289_260_case_study.md`: curated explanation of the Sage/Mordell-Weil
  delta `289/260` run and intersection audit.
- `final_checkpoint_summary.md`: final first-stage summary and non-claims.
- `sage_fiber_rank_report.md`: latest Sage fiber rank/generator and exact
  bridge verification report.
- `scaled_sage_fiber_report.md`: scaled-run summary, including the completed
  `max_multiple=100`, `combo_bound=20` checkpoint and notes on larger attempts
  that did not complete.
- `fiber_intersections_delta_289_260.md`: exact `U_B`, `U_C`, `U_D`
  intersection audit for delta `289/260`.
- `delta_scan_lite_report.md`: small multi-delta scan for generic sparsity.
- `defect_summary_100000.md` and `defect_summary_1000000.md`: K2,2 seed scan
  summaries.

Reports are finite computational records.  They must not be cited as proof of
non-existence unless a separate mathematical argument is supplied.
